Create two animated gifs using Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop

Exercise 1: Blink
Complete the exercise started in your lab section that uses the eye drawing created with Adobe Illustrator from homework #6 to make the drawing blink. Copy your eye from Illustrator into Photoshop and follow the steps in the examples above to create the blink. Experiment with the number of frames and fps to get the speed of the blink you like.

Exercise 2: Cut Up Cinema

  • Create photographs of yourself or friend(s). Be sure to photograph against a highcontrast solid background to make it easier to “pull off” the figure.
  • In Photoshop cut the person out of the image to create a digital puppet.
  • Use Puppet Warp Tool to animate the cropped photography.
  • Duplicate your person into various frames.
  • In each frame create variations of the person using the Puppet Warp tool.
  • Open the Timeline panel and create a “Frame Animation”.
  • Present each layer on different frames to create an animation of you or a friend in various positions.
  • Add background layers, more people or text… Have fun with it.

Post both gifs to your website.
