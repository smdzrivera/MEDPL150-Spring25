Create a new Illustrator document and use the following preferences: Profile: Art & Illustration; Size: 1920 x 1080 pt; Orientation: Landscape; Artboards: 1; Bleed: 0; Advanced Options - Color Mode: RGB Color; Raster Effects: High (300 points per inch); Preview Mode: Default

Design Elements in Photomontage - from Illustrator to Photoshop Using at minimum 1 repeating shape with 1 color create a design that will be incorporated into your Photomontage (view the Grid, Rulers & Guides and Smart Guides to help you). You may elect to add more elements such as text and other shapes to try different designs. Consider using symmetry or asymmetry as well as other design concepts.

Select and copy the vector designs and/or text from Illustrator and paste it into Photoshop Photomontage Document as a Smart Object. In Photoshop, resize, rotate and transform as necessary to integrate the vector design. Post the final photomontage to your website.
