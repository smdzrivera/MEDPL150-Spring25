This is a two-part project split over two homework assignments that expand your Photoshop toolset and introduces you to Illustrator to combine Photomontage with Vector Design.

As we saw in the lecture, photomontage can be used to create political commentary, cultural critique, express the subconscious or create self-portraits. For this assignment, create two photomontages that use masking, at least one should also use a blending mode to present a message. Brainstorm a political, social or ecological issue that interests you and utilize this issue to inform you images and design.

Each photomontage should combine at least three images; however, you may want to use many more. Make precise selections and use masking to combine different elements of your image. Use non-destructive editing techniques in your process. Like the images we saw in class, your photomontage should have a concept that drives the selection and juxtapositions of images. Review the discussion of blending modes in the lecture and apply the techniques you learn in the lab sections to think about how you may use this technique creatively. In your pieces, try to use as many images as possible that you have shot, are in the public domain, or have a Creative Commons license, one resource is: Wikimedia Commons.

Both of your finished images should be 5”x7” and posted on your website as jpg images. Please also include a screenshot of both of your PSD working files including an expanded view of your layers panel. On your website under these images, write a two-paragraph explanation of the concepts you are exploring and what techniques you employed to achieve the results and how you used non-destructive editing techniques in your process. Describe which blending mode(s) you chose and why. Make sure to also discuss the source of your images, are they from an online site or a photo you made?
